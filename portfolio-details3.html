Hob assistant

Project information

  • Category: Supervised Learning
  • Project: Hob assistant repository (in italian)
  • Demo: Link to Binder

In this project, I was part of an University Team and our goal was to gather pictures of hobs (4 stations) with pots and a pan and then build a CNN capable of counting the number of pots (max 2) and confirming the presence of a pan. The final model achieved an accuracy of 89%. Our team also developed a smartphone demo that allows to try the model in real-time hobs.
