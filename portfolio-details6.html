Employees future prediction

Project information

  • Category: EDA - Supervised Learning
  • Project: Link to Kaggle

In this project, I worked on a dataset populated by employees data of a given company. For each employee, there are several variables and one of them (LeavesOrNot) is a binary attribute that indicates wheter the employee left the company 2 years later after being hired or not. I performed an EDA to learn more about the company's employees and to find any problem that would harm the employees or the company. Then, I modeled an XGBOOST classifier to predict the LeavesOrNot variable, with a final accuracy of 86% on the test set.
